Experiment 01

Sudarshan Chakra AR

sudarshan_chakra_ar.py

Real-time AR overlay of the mythological Sudarshan Chakra — weapon of Lord Krishna — rendered as a lateral spinning disc on your index finger's distal phalanx. The chakra auto-scales to your finger, tilts with it, and glows with a divine cosmic aura.

Lateral disc perspective Pulsing divine aura Rotating light rays Particle sparkles Auto-scales to finger Tracks finger tilt
$ python3 sudarshan_chakra_ar.py

Experiment 02

Finger Connect AR

finger_connect_ar.py

Hold both hands up with fingertips facing each other — the space between your fingers comes alive with energy effects. Tracks all 10 fingertips across both hands simultaneously with persistent motion trails and additive glow blending.
